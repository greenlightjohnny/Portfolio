My Story

I graduated from George Mason University with a BA in International Relations and promptly put my degree to good use climbing radio and cell towers around the country as a tower technician.

After a few years I decided to make my hobby of skydiving my full time career. I was lucky enough to work in Australia, New Zealand, China, and Germany while taking thousands of people for their first skydive. A string of parachute malfunctions, airplane engine issues, and friends getting injured led me to the decision to find a new career.

My previous web development experience was editing the HTML in Expedia bookings to print out fake "proof of onward travel" itineraries, so the transition has been pretty easy so far.
